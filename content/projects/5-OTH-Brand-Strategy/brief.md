## About OTH

Managed four brands simultaneously: **Amoree** (apparel), **Bellissimo Home Cafe**, **Sutra Coffee**, and **Sequoia Cafe** - each with a distinct voice and target audience, all operating in Jaipur's competitive F&B and lifestyle scene.

## The Challenge

Four brands. One manager. Every brand needed its own content calendar, visual identity, event presence, and growth strategy - all running in parallel.

## Execution

I led account management for all four brands, using comprehensive market insights to craft strategies tailored to each brand's identity. From social media content refinement to Instagram SEO to email marketing, every channel was optimized for visibility and engagement.

Immersive events served as pivotal community touchpoints:
- **Cupping sessions** at Sutra - intimate, experience-driven events building a coffee community
- **Bellissimo Home Pop-up** - 600+ attendees, lifestyle brand positioning
- **Food for Thought festival** - cultural event expanding brand reach across Jaipur

Key work across all brands:
- Strategic content planning and Instagram SEO
- Meta ads and performance marketing
- Influencer collaborations tailored to each brand's audience
- End-to-end event planning and execution
- Email marketing campaigns

## Outcomes

- **~8 Lakhs in offline event sales** (Amoree)
- **600+ event attendees** (Bellissimo)
- **13% follower growth** across managed accounts
- **1,000+ footfall** driven to Sutra locations
- **20,000+ organic reach** generated for Sequoia
