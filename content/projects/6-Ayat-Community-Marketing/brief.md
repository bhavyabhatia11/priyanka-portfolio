## About Ayat

Ayat is a community-led brand dedicated to Indian classical music - creating spaces where artists and audiences connect through live concerts, intimate performances, and cultural storytelling.

## The Challenge

Starting from zero. No community, no content, no channels. The brief was to build a brand and audience from the ground up for a niche but passionate segment of Indian culture.

## The Work

I built Ayat's brand and community architecture from scratch - developing the content strategy, event presence, and multi-channel distribution across Instagram, email, and WhatsApp.

Every touchpoint was designed to feel like an invitation, not a broadcast. The community grew because people felt they belonged to something, not just that they were being marketed to.

- End-to-end marketing across events, email, WhatsApp, and Instagram
- 9+ offline events executed and promoted
- Brand identity and content strategy developed from zero
- Community engagement systems (WhatsApp groups, email sequences)

## Outcomes

- **0 → 7,000+ community members** built from scratch
- **9+ offline events** successfully executed
- A loyal, engaged audience for Indian classical music
- Demonstrated that niche communities can be built with intention and patience
