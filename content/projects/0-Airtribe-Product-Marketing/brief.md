## About Airtribe

Airtribe is an edtech platform helping professionals upskill through cohort-based courses and community learning. The platform sits at the intersection of education, community, and career growth.

## The Work

As Product Marketer, I own GTM research, community scaling, monetization strategy, and content production. I've conducted 400+ customer interviews and surveys to understand user behavior, sharpen messaging, and build a bank of UGC that feeds directly into channel creative.

I launched Airtribe's **GenAI program** - a new course product that now drives ₹50L+ in monthly revenue - handling everything from market research and pricing to launch strategy and community activation.

I also created and scaled a **paid cohort deferral program** that generates ₹25L+ in incremental monthly revenue, turning a potential churn moment into a monetization opportunity.

I'm also the **on-camera face of the brand** - producing Reels, Shorts, long-form videos, podcasts, and testimonials end-to-end (research, scripting, shooting, editing). I lead **founder-led content** too: scripting, shooting, and publishing for the founder's LinkedIn, growing their profile from 5K → 12K.

I ran **competitive analysis** across 4–5 edtech players (benchmarking pricing, brochures, mentors, course structure), built sales enablement materials and objection-handling frameworks, contributing to ~10% enrolment growth. A/B testing on messaging and creatives runs continuously to optimise conversion.

On social growth (7% average engagement rate):
- Instagram: 15K → 40K
- YouTube: 8K → 15K
- LinkedIn brand page: 30K → 48K | Founder profile: 5K → 12K
- Peak organic reach: **21.1M views/month**

I run email marketing campaigns via **MailModo** across the learner lifecycle, generating 4K+ leads/month from email alone.

I've also led 70+ offline and online events with 7,000+ total attendees, building a community that shows up - not just online but in real life.
