## Airtribe Content Marketing Assignment

A content marketing strategy for Airtribe's **GenAI for PMs** program - designed to drive curiosity, signups, and follower growth among a niche audience of PMs, devs, and analysts.

## What's Inside

- **Verbatims & Research** - Real quotes from PMs at Milaap, Zomato showing the pain points GenAI solves
- **LinkedIn Content Copy** - A scroll-stopping post written from PM pain points, not product features
- **Bonus Post Idea** - A narrative-driven story post following "Swetank" before and after GenAI
- **Short-Form Video Concepts** - Two reel concepts: result-first utility and peer testimonial format
- **Growth Strategy** - Three levers: "Show Don't Sell" content, relatable skits & memes, real alumni use cases
- **Success Metrics** - Engagement, content health, conversion, and reach metrics framework

## The Approach

Lead with jaw-dropping results, then tease the "how." Real workflows over hype. Build a community of PMs who are done surviving on caffeine and context-switching.
