## About Parina

Parina is a jewelry brand looking to revitalize its market presence and connect with a broader, more engaged customer base. The goal: significantly boost reach, engagement, and drive sales growth through smarter brand positioning.

## The Approach

Identifying the outsized influence of celebrities on luxury purchasing decisions, we built a campaign strategy around prominent women celebrities whose values and aesthetic matched Parina's brand ethos. The focus was on building aspirational positioning - making Parina feel like a brand people wanted to be seen wearing.

## Execution

- Deep market research to identify the right celebrity profiles aligned with the brand
- Strategic celebrity partnerships built around luxury storytelling
- Social media management and content planning across platforms
- Influencer marketing campaigns to expand organic reach
- Performance ad campaigns to maximize ROI

## Outcomes

- **22.2% increase** in brand reach and engagement
- Elevated brand profile within the luxury jewelry segment
- Deeper customer loyalty and a more connected audience
- Higher average ticket prices driven by brand desirability
