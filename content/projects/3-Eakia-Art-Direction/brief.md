## About Eakia

Eakia is an apparel brand with a bold, eccentric identity. The challenge: translate this personality into a cohesive digital presence that would attract the right audience and drive genuine engagement.

## The Brief

This campaign - internally called **NIGHTAMORE** - was a bold creative pivot for the brand. The brief was to build a visual identity from the ground up: art direct the shoots, coordinate the talent, produce the content, and back it with media spend to make sure it reached the right people.

## Execution

Ensuring consistency and cohesive storytelling was the core focus. I coordinated with models, photographers, and stylists to bring the NIGHTAMORE vision to life. Alongside the creative work, I managed targeted Meta ads to maximize visibility with the right audiences.

- Themed photoshoots with detailed art direction
- Full coordination with models, photographers, and stylists
- Consistent visual storytelling across all content touchpoints
- Targeted Meta ad campaigns for reach and visibility

## Outcomes

- **2,000+ new Instagram followers** gained
- Significant increase in sales, brand visibility, and engagement
- Successfully translated the brand's eccentric identity into a compelling digital presence
- Demonstrated the power of strategic planning paired with a strong visual theme
