## About Amoree

Amoree is an apparel brand aiming to enhance its market presence and create direct, meaningful connections with customers. The campaign focused on offline events - exhibitions and pop-ups - to showcase products and build lasting brand relationships.

## The Approach

Starting with a thorough analysis of Amoree's market position and customer preferences, I identified offline interactions as the highest-value channel. The strategy: immersive events that turned browsers into buyers, backed by omnichannel marketing to amplify reach before, during, and after each event.

## Execution

I personally oversaw logistics for each event while spearheading omnichannel marketing efforts to maximize reach. Influencer collaborations and compelling content kept the buzz alive across digital touchpoints.

- End-to-end event planning and logistics for exhibitions and pop-ups
- Omnichannel marketing to bridge offline events with online presence
- Influencer collaborations to drive buzz and foot traffic
- Compelling content creation to support campaign momentum
- Account management to maintain brand consistency throughout

## Outcomes

- **₹8+ Lakhs in sales** from offline events alone
- Elevated brand visibility and customer engagement
- Direct customer interaction reinforced brand loyalty and growth
- Proven effectiveness of combining offline and online strategies
