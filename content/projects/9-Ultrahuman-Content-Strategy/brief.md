## Ultrahuman Content Strategy

A two-part content marketing assignment for **Ultrahuman** - the smart ring brand competing with Oura, Whoop, and Garmin.

## Part 1 - Instagram Audit & Content Buckets

- **Audit findings** - Ultrahuman posts less frequently than all competitors; 70% of content is informational with no entertainment or aspirational content
- **User Personas** - Athletes, tech-focused individuals, minimalists, and impulse buyers
- **5 Content Buckets** - Inspirational & Aspirational, Entertainment & Interactive, Social Proof & Community, Brand Storytelling & BTS, Seasonal & Event-Centric
- **Execution Plan** - 5+ posts/week, diverse formats, long-form repurposed into Reels, data-driven iteration

## Part 2 - Women's Day Campaign

- **Why Women's Day** - Women are a fast-growing Ultrahuman segment; performance over appreciation messaging
- **Breaking Barriers** - Dynamic montage of women in elite fitness spaces (ref: Nike, Adidas)
- **Real Women, Real Stories** - UGC + feature reel format (ref: Apple, Whoop)
- **Fun & Witty** - "A Ring That Pays More Attention Than Your Boyfriend" carousel concept
