## About Milaap

Milaap is India's largest online crowdfunding platform - enabling anyone across the country to raise funds for healthcare, education, sports, disaster relief, and personal causes. Founded in 2010, Milaap has pioneered person-to-person giving in India.

## The Work

As Marketing Operations Associate, I sourced and managed 70+ fundraising campaigns, published 36+ stories, and raised over ₹6 crore for medical and non-medical causes across India. I owned end-to-end campaign management - from story sourcing and stakeholder communication to creative production and social media distribution.

I handled PR and logistics for 7 major medical awareness events, bringing in 2,000+ attendees and securing government support from Karnataka and Tamil Nadu. On social media, I curated 200+ creatives and drove a 20% follower increase - generating six-figure revenue on a sub-₹50K ad budget. I also led influencer collaborations with 50+ macro and micro creators, producing high-engagement face reels that expanded audience reach significantly.

I collaborated with product and engineering on feature launches - employer matching, verified causes, campaign updates - owning GTM strategy and messaging end-to-end. I also managed the launch of 2 handbooks, driving a **7% increase in overall fundraising conversion**.

I ran **in-app and push notification campaigns** to re-engage donors and boost campaign visibility: **18% average open rate** and **6% click-through rate** across 50+ campaigns.
